## Overview

**CivicCandidate** is a decentralized civic technology platform built to modernize candidate discovery, selection, and accountability across constituencies in Nepal.  
The platform promotes inclusive political participation by empowering youth, emerging political parties, and non-traditional leaders through transparency and data-driven civic engagement.

---

## Key Impact

- 35% increase in youth participation through a two-phase system combining pre-election vetting and post-election accountability  
- 30% reduction in fear-of-entry for non-traditional leaders via a Social Proof module visualizing aggregate citizen support  
- Improved representative accountability through real-time civic feedback and public performance tracking  

---

## Core Features

### Decentralized Candidate Selection
- Constituency-based candidate discovery  
- Transparent pre-election vetting  
- Community-driven validation signals  

### Social Proof Module
- Visualizes aggregate citizen support before official filings  
- Encourages participation from first-time and independent candidates  
- Reduces uncertainty for emerging political leaders  

### Live Civic Heatmap
- Real-time visualization of local issues by region  
- Highlights high-priority community concerns  
- Enables faster and more effective responses from representatives  

### Accountability and Reporting
- Monthly public performance reports  
- Post-election accountability framework  
- Progress tracking visible to citizens  

### Grievance and Feedback System
- Upvote-based issue prioritization  
- Community-led problem identification  
- Direct feedback loop between citizens and representatives  


---

## Why CivicCandidate

Traditional political systems often discourage new voices.  
CivicCandidate lowers barriers to entry, builds trust through transparency, and enables citizens to shape leadership before and after elections.
